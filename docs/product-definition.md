# FlowNexus

FlowNexus is a team workflow intelligence platform that helps organizations track employee productivity through structured daily reporting and analytics dashboards.

## Problem

Managers often lack visibility into what employees accomplish daily. Updates are scattered across chats, emails, or meetings, making productivity tracking difficult.

## Solution

FlowNexus provides a centralized platform where employees submit daily work reports and managers can monitor team productivity through dashboards and analytics.

## Users

### Employee
- submit daily work reports
- view their report history

### Manager
- view team reports
- monitor team productivity

### Admin
- create teams
- manage employees
- assign managers

## Core Features

- authentication system
- role-based access
- daily reporting
- manager dashboards
- team management
